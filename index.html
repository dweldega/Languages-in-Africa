Poverty and HDI in Africa


The goal is to create a geo mapping of the African continent and gather data that can be used to visualize the dependencies of how population growth is affected by poverty, inflation and HDI over a certain time period. We plan on using a slider to show the differnt years and radio buttons to switch between the different variable.
